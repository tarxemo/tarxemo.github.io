Extras

Explore all the extra benefits and facilities available at Pius School that make your experience unique and enriching.

Getting Around the School

Walking and cycling are the most popular ways to navigate the Pius School campus. With state-of-the-art security and 24-hour emergency services, it’s a safe and convenient environment for everyone. Private and public transport options are also well-organized to suit your needs.

Campus Lifestyle

Life at Pius School is vibrant and diverse. From entertainment to extracurricular activities and even religious pursuits, there’s something for everyone. There's never a dull moment on campus.

Student Affairs

Students play an active role in organizing activities through their own organization, supported by the school administration. From social events to sports and other entertainment, the organization is the bridge between students and administration.

Religious Life

The campus includes prayer halls for all major religions, fostering a respectful and inclusive environment for everyone.

Studying at Pius Made Easy!

Our community is committed to providing quality education at competitive rates, with flexible installment payment options. Join our community and experience a kind and supportive educational environment.

How It Works

  1. Collect the registration form from our school campus (see the contact page for directions).
  2. Undergo assessment to qualify for your selected level (O-level or A-level).
  3. Pay school fees (in full or installments).
  4. Begin your academic journey at Pius School!
