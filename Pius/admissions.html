Admission

Entry to Pius Secondary School is based on merit and is very competitive due to the high number of students seeking admission. Below are the guidelines and requirements for various admission categories.

Admissions to ‘O’ Level

  • Applicants must have completed seven years of primary education (Grade 7).
  • Shortlisted candidates must pass an entrance examination.
  • Form I-IV admissions take place in December, based on availability.
  • Entrance exams align with the applicant’s desired entry level.

Admissions to A-Level

  • Applicants must have completed Form IV and passed at least three subjects.
  • Selected candidates will be contacted via the address provided in the application form.
  • Admission letters will be sent to the shortlisted candidates.

Mid-Term Entry

Students applying mid-way through the school calendar will be admitted depending on the availability of space. However, all applicants must meet the standard entry requirements.

Admission for Non-Tanzanian Students

  • Admission procedures are the same as for Tanzanian students.
  • Foreign students must complete necessary immigration formalities.
  • Confirmation of immigration formalities must be submitted to the school administration before studies begin.

Admission for Students from Other Regions

Our school provides accommodation for students from regions outside Dar es Salaam. If you're applying from upcountry, rest assured we’ll provide you with suitable living arrangements.

Admission Overview

Our admission process is designed to select the most deserving students while maintaining inclusivity. Whether you're applying for O-level, A-level, mid-term, or as a non-Tanzanian student, we ensure a smooth and transparent admission process.
