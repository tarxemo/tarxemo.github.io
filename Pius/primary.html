About Pius Pre-Primary School

Pius Pre-Primary School is committed to providing a safe, nurturing, and inspiring environment for children at the early stages of their academic journey. Our mission is to lay the foundation for future learning and development by providing high-quality education that promotes curiosity, creativity, and emotional growth. Located in the heart of Kongowe, we offer an enriched curriculum for our students, ensuring they receive the best start possible for their educational journey.

Mission & Vision

At Pius Pre-Primary School, our mission is to provide a secure, engaging, and stimulating environment where young children can learn and grow. We believe in holistic education that nurtures both the mind and character, ensuring children develop a love for learning. We aim to equip students with the skills they need for their future education and personal development.

Our vision is to be recognized as a leading pre-primary school that fosters creativity, inclusivity, and academic excellence, ensuring that each child feels valued and supported.

Our Curriculum

Our curriculum at Pius Pre-Primary School is designed to nurture curiosity, creativity, and social skills. We focus on developing key areas such as literacy, numeracy, arts, physical education, and socio-emotional learning. We use a child-centered approach, where the learning experiences are tailored to meet each child's individual needs.

Literacy

Focus on phonics, basic reading and writing skills, and early language development.

Numeracy

Introduction to numbers, counting, shapes, and basic arithmetic.

Art & Creativity

Encouraging self-expression through drawing, painting, and crafting activities.
