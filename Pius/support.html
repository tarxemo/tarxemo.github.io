Questions & Answers

Joining Pius Secondary School is very simple. You have to first visit our school campus to collect your registration form if you are a new student. After returning a duly filled form, our staff will assess your application. If you meet minimum entry qualifications, you will be selected to join, and we will notify you by sending a joining instruction letter to the address you provided.

Yes, we accommodate both O-level and A-level students.
Pius Secondary School is built on about 10 acres of land. We have enough hostels to accommodate any number of students. Pius secondary school is a boarding scholar only.
Our school is based on the Tanzania Ministry of Education and Vocational Training curriculum. We offer all subjects directed by the ministry, including Civics, History, Geography, Kiswahili, English, Literature in English, Basic Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Commerce, Bookkeeping, and Information and Computer Studies (ICS).
