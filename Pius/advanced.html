About Pius Advanced Level Secondary School

Pius Advanced Level Secondary School offers an enriched and comprehensive educational experience for students seeking to pursue their dreams at the tertiary level. With a rigorous curriculum designed to prepare students for university and professional careers, our school is committed to providing both academic excellence and personal growth. We emphasize critical thinking, leadership, and holistic development.

Dr. Samuel W. Amani

Dr. Samuel W. Amani has been leading Pius Advanced Level Secondary School for the last 15 years, with a Ph.D. in Education Leadership. His approach to leadership focuses on empowering students, teachers, and the community. Dr. Amani is known for his academic rigor, nurturing environment, and commitment to preparing students for higher education and the future workforce.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide a challenging and holistic education that prepares students for successful university careers and impactful professional lives. We believe in shaping well-rounded individuals who are not only academically strong but also socially responsible, creative, and innovative thinkers.

Our vision is to be a leading secondary school recognized for producing exceptional graduates who excel academically and professionally in their chosen fields. We are committed to empowering students to achieve their dreams and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Curriculum

Pius Advanced Level Secondary School offers an in-depth curriculum focused on preparing students for their academic futures. Students are guided through specialized study tracks in the sciences, arts, and humanities, allowing them to explore their strengths and interests.

Science Track

Focused on advanced subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics.

Humanities Track

Including subjects like History, Geography, and Literature, preparing students for higher learning.

Languages & Arts Track

Emphasis on creative arts, languages, and communication skills, aimed at fostering cultural and intellectual growth.

Our Facilities

The school is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities that provide a conducive learning environment. Our facilities cater to both academic and extracurricular activities, ensuring that every student has the tools they need to succeed in their studies and personal development.

  • Modern classrooms with smart boards and projectors
  • Fully-equipped science laboratories
  • Art and music studios for creative arts students
  • Sports facilities, including a football field, tennis courts, and a gymnasium
  • A well-stocked library and digital learning center
  • Computer and ICT labs
  • Boarding facilities with 24-hour security and care
