Studying at Pius Made Easy!

Our community is highly committed to giving all our students the best quality education they deserve. We have made our rates very competitive, and you can pay in installments to ensure that all potential students can get the best quality education from Pius Secondary School. You will be treated with kindness like nowhere else. Join our community today!

This is How It Works

  1. Collect the registration form from our school campus. Visit our Contact Page to get directions and phone numbers.
  2. Be assessed for qualification at your chosen level (O-level or A-level).
  3. Pay school fees (full or installment).
  4. Get started!
