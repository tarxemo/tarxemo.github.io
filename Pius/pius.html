Our Core Values

At Pius Secondary School, we value the following principles that guide our educational journey.

Community

We foster a strong sense of community within our school, encouraging teamwork, collaboration, and respect.

Academic Excellence

We strive to provide the highest quality education and ensure academic excellence for every student.

Leadership

We prepare our students to be the leaders of tomorrow, fostering critical thinking, innovation, and independence.

About Pius Secondary School

Pius Secondary School is committed to providing high-quality education based on the curriculum set by the Ministry of Education and Vocational Training. Our mission is to nurture future leaders who excel academically, socially, and emotionally.

Located in Kongowe Mwisho (Mbagala), we offer both O-Level and A-Level education with a focus on comprehensive development through diverse extracurricular activities and academic rigor.
