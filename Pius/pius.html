About Pius Secondary School

Pius Secondary School is committed to providing high-quality education based on the curriculum set by the Ministry of Education and Vocational Training. Our mission is to nurture future leaders who excel academically, socially, and emotionally.

Located in Kongowe Mwisho (Mbagala), we offer both O-Level and A-Level education with a focus on comprehensive development through diverse extracurricular activities and academic rigor.
