About Pius Ordinary Level Secondary School

Pius Ordinary Level Secondary School stands as a beacon of academic excellence and personal development. With a diverse curriculum and a commitment to quality education, we prepare students for both the Tanzanian Certificate of Secondary Education (CSEE) and for success in life beyond the classroom. Established with a focus on high academic standards and character development, we aim to nurture well-rounded, future-ready individuals who are confident in their academic abilities and personal character.

Mr. Joseph M. Mbwana

Mr. Joseph M. Mbwana is the current headmaster of Pius Ordinary Level Secondary School, having served in this role for over 12 years. With a wealth of experience in secondary education and a passion for student development, Mr. Mbwana leads the school with dedication and a focus on academic success. He is known for his inclusive leadership style, emphasizing teamwork, student well-being, and a strong academic foundation.

Mission & Vision

At Pius Ordinary Level Secondary School, our mission is to provide quality education that empowers students to excel academically, socially, and personally. We are dedicated to fostering critical thinking, personal integrity, and social responsibility in every student.

Our vision is to be a center of academic excellence where students thrive in a nurturing environment that promotes creativity, discipline, and lifelong learning, preparing them for global challenges and opportunities.

Our Curriculum

We offer a comprehensive and balanced curriculum that prepares students for both the CSEE exams and their future careers. Our curriculum includes core subjects in the sciences, humanities, and languages, with a focus on practical knowledge and skills development. Students are also encouraged to explore their interests through extracurricular activities.

Mathematics

Focus on problem-solving, algebra, geometry, and calculus.

Sciences

Subjects include Biology, Physics, and Chemistry, with laboratory-based learning.

Languages

We offer Kiswahili, English, and French, with a focus on reading and communication skills.

Our Facilities

Pius Ordinary Level Secondary School offers a variety of modern facilities designed to support both academic and extracurricular activities. These include:

  • Spacious classrooms with multimedia capabilities
  • Fully-equipped science and computer laboratories
  • Well-stocked library with a variety of books and online resources
  • Sports fields for athletics, football, and basketball
  • A fully functional school hall for events and assemblies
  • Canteen providing nutritious meals for students
  • Secure dormitories for boarding students
