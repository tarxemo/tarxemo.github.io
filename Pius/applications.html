Application Process

Applying to our school is straightforward:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (e.g., transcripts, certificates).
  3. Attend an entrance examination or interview (if applicable).
  4. Receive admission confirmation.

Application Requirements

  • Completed application form.
  • Copies of previous academic records.
  • A recommendation letter (if applicable).
  • Passport-sized photographs.

Application Fees

A non-refundable application fee is required to process your application. Please contact the school for the latest fee structure.
