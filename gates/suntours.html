Pemba Creek Safari

3 - 4 hours

We will sail in calm waters along the extremely picturesque Gando creek; here you will see mangroves, flora, and beautiful birds as the dhow sails. You will be visiting a local village and during the visit, youll be entertained by local drums and you can join us in singing some African songs!

Pemba Sunset Cruise

Full day tour

Whether on honeymoon, with your family or by yourself, going on a sunset cruise in a traditional sailing dhow in Pemba island is the perfect way to wind down the day. Come with your partner to turn up the romance or with your friends and family to see the island of Pemba right from the oceans perspective! One can savour a glass of chilled wine, cocktail, beer or soft drink as you watch the villages a bit far from the Ocean.
